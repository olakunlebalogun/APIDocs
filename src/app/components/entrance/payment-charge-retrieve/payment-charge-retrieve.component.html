Payment Charge Retrieval

This is the endpoint responsible for calculating the charges associated with the transaction amount.

Calculating The Charges

The endpoint takes only two parameters, namely :

  • The transaction amount : this is the amount the merchant is willing to borrow.
  • Period: The maximum number of month(s) the said amount will be paid back.

For instance, if the amount is #1000, and the charges will be 15% of the amount. Therefore, the total amount to be paid back will be #1150
i.e 115%.
