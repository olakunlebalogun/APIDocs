Payment Installment

This endpoint helps to spread payment over a period of time, mainly

  • One time payment
  • Two-time payment
  • Three-time payment
Installment Plan

For a customer to choose the duration of repayment, the following parameters must be supplied to the request

  • Ref: This is a unique number for a particular transaction made by customer.
  • selectedInstallment:The expected value here is 1,2 or 3. This tells the number of times a customer is willing to repay.
