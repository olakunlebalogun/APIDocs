Pay Merchant

This is the endpoint responsible for processing payment of a merchant

Pay a Merchant

For you to proceed with the payment , you will need the following request:

  • Account name
  • Account number
  • Amount
  • Bank name
  • Item name
  • Merchant email
  • Phone number
  • User's transaction pin
  • User's email
